Fred Hollows is a legendary Australian whose legacy continues to change the world today.

Fred said it was “obscene to let people go blind when they don’t have to.” He was a visionary who fought the injustice of avoidable blindness – one person at a time.

Fred was taken from us too soon. But the work he started all those years ago showed us that one person really can make a difference. And so can you, by becoming a Visionary for The Fred Hollows Foundation today.

We’re asking people like you to sign up to become a Visionary for The Fred Hollows Foundation today, so that we can keep our promise to Fred and end avoidable blindness around the world. By becoming a Visionary you will fight the injustice of avoidable blindness and help finish the job Fred started.

Vision changes everything.

How it increases happiness

Monthly giving is the most effective way to donate – we count on your monthly donations to be able to plan ahead and provide support to people living with avoidable blindness in over 25 countries around the world.

Your monthly donation enables us to train doctors who then train others, build clinics and work with governments to reach those who no one else can. Your money goes further with a monthly donation by restoring sight to more people.

Steps to complete the task

Go to our website and and choose a donation options. You choose how much you give and the donation is automatically deducted from your nominated account on the 15th or 28th of every month. This means fewer administrative tasks for us too – so it keeps admin costs down. You are in complete control of your giving and can modify anytime.
